My Project

A website which asks the user to input different parameters such as Glucose, blood pressure, Insulin, BMI, age, etc. Then by using Deep Learning, the model is being trained upon different data set symptoms which confirm diabetes and we can predict the probability of that individual to have diabetes. If the probability is high above a certain limit then the site will redirect the patient to the possible medications he/she could take to control their sugar in the body.

Since, this model can predict only diabetes, similarly in the future we can also add symptoms about other diseases and illnesses as well, and can make a website combined with the prediction rate of those illnesses and their possible solutions

  • Link: https://github.com/ShubhMittal44/Diapredict.git
  • Date: June 2021
  • Teck Stack: Web Development and Machine Learning

My Project

There is increasing evidence that gardening provides substantial physical and mental health benefits. We envision a future where the vast majority of people lead a healthy life in tandem with nature by actively taking part in protecting our environment.

Our project “Know you Roots” incorporates buying and selling options of a vast variety of plants listed by local nurseries. Not only does it give the user the advantage of purchasing from home but also includes added features like newsletter feeds, a question answer forum, a database of information of plants and their climatic conditions, pesticides etc. Finally, our website also gives a chance for the gardeners to sell their surplus produce to interested local customers, thereby promoting organic farming which has a great demand due to their health benefits.

  • Teck Stack: Frontend Development, Backend Development, Machine Learning
